Creating Data Refresh: 10

1. On the Power BI Desktop ribbon, click "Transform Data."

2. Click on "New Source" and choose "Blank Query." and Name the query 'Last Updated' in the Properties.

3. In the DAX formula bar, type 'Last Updated= DateTime.LocalNow()'.

4. Click on "Convert to Table" when the Last Updated table pops up.

5. Right-click on the column header and choose "Rename" to rename the column.

6. Click on "Close & Apply" to save the changes and close the Power Query Editor.

7. Save the Power BI file to preserve the added Last Updated information.
